### <a name="Form">`Form`</a>: formal register

The polite register is usually meant for communication with people of higher social status than the one of the speaker.
In Beja, the feature « polite » applies to the use of 1PL pronouns instead of 1SG pronouns.

#### Examples
- _doːr han / hoːb <b>=uːn</b> -ej iraːnaj // oː-= meːk ʔam -a =b =wa_ "Grand-father, gosh, have you ever ridden a donkey?"
