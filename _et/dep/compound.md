title: 'compound'
shortdef: 'compound'
---

The dependency relation compound is used for annotating numbers written with several words or several sequences of digits or combinations of them.

~~~ sdparse
60 000
compound(60-1, 000-2)
~~

~~~sdparse
kaks tuhat
compound(kaks-1, tuhat-2)
~~~
