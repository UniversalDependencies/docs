title: 'vocative'
shortdef: 'vocative'
---

The `vocative` relation is used to mark dialogue participant addressed
in text (common in emails and newsgroup postings). The relation links
the addressee's name to its host sentence.

~~~ sdparse
Guys , take it easy!
vocative(take, Guys)
~~~
