Treebank Statistics (UD_Romanian)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

1665 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 966 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 680 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ro-pos/VERB (1471; 12% instances), ro-pos/AUX (194; 2% instances).
