### Description

Uralic languages follow universal rules for subordinating conjunctions. The
comparative conjunction is tagged as SCONJ.

#### Examples

* [fi] _että_ "that", _koska_ "because", _jos_ "if"
* [fi] _parempi <b>kuin</b>_ "better <b>than</b>"
