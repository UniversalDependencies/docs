## amod: adjectival modifier

An adjectival modifier of a noun is any adjectival phrase that serves
to modify the meaning of the noun.

~~~ sdparse
Sam eats red meat
amod(meat, red)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Sam took out  a 3 million dollar loan
amod(loan, dollar)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Sam took out  a $ 3 million loan
amod(loan, $)
~~~
