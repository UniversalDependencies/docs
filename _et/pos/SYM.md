Definition


A symbol is a word-like entity that differs from ordinary words by form, function, or both.<br/>
Mail and web addresses are also tagged also SYM.<br/>

Examples:<br/>
<i>§, %, +, =, 11°, 25°53’</i><br/>
<i>info.euroopaliikumine.ee</i>
