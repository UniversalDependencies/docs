---
layout: relation
title: 'det'
shortdef: 'determiner'
---

A determiner is the relation between the head of an NP and its determiner. 

~~~ sdparse
Den engelska parken är vacker \n The English park is beautiful
det(parken, Den)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Vilken bok föredrar du ? \n Which book do you prefer ?
det(bok, Vilken)
~~~
