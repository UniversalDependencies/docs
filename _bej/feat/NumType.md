### <a name="Ord">`Ord`</a>: ordinal number or corresponding interrogative / relative / indefinite / demonstrative word

Used to distinguish numbers from the cardinal numerals which are ordinals stem with an added suffix. 

#### Examples
- _areː <b>mali -a</b> doːr ingiːdhoːm /_ "Then, when he throws it a <b>second</b>(two + suffix) time"
