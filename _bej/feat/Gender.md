`Gender` is a lexical feature of [nouns](u-pos/NOUN) and inflectional feature
of other parts of speech ([pronouns](u-pos/PRON), [determiners](u-pos/DET)) that marks agreement with
nouns and indicates syntactically agreement on the verb.


### <a name="Masc">`Masc`</a>: masculine gender

Nouns denoting males are usually masculine. Other nouns may be also
grammatically masculine, without any relation to sex.

#### Examples
- _i=_ `DET` Masc
- _dhaj_ `NOUN` Masc "People"

### <a name="Fem">`Fem`</a>: feminine gender

Nouns denoting females are usually feminine. Other nouns may be also
grammatically feminine, without any relation to sex.

#### Examples
- _ti-_ `PRON` Fem 3SG 
- _halla_ `NOUN` Fem "Village"
