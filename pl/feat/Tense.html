Treebank Statistics (UD_Polish)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

11423 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5319 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2328 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: pl-pos/VERB (10176; 12% instances), pl-pos/AUX (1247; 1% instances).

VERB

10176 pl-pos/VERB tokens (73% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Negative=EMPTY (9887; 97%), Voice=EMPTY (9887; 97%), Case=EMPTY (9887; 97%), VerbForm=Fin (9697; 95%), Mood=Ind (9697; 95%), Number=Sing (7489; 74%), Animacy=EMPTY (6664; 65%), Aspect=Imp (6531; 64%), Person=EMPTY (5717; 56%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Past (5243; 52% of non-empty Tense): był, było, była, miał, chciał, miała, były, udało, mógł, mieli
  • Pres (4933; 48% of non-empty Tense): jest, ma, są, może, dziękuję, mają, wiem, mam, proszę, jestem
  • EMPTY (3777): to, można, być, trzeba, powiedzieć, wiadomo, trudno, widać, zrobić, warto
