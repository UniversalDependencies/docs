The dependency type `compound` in UD Japanese is used for noun compounds and verb compounds.

~~~ sdparse
構文 解析 \n syntax analysis
compound(解析, 構文)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
泳ぎ 着く \n swim reach
compound(着く, 泳ぎ)
~~~
