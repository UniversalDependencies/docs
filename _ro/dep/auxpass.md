layout: relation
title: 'auxpass'
shortdef: 'passive auxiliary'
---

A passive auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause which contains the passive information.

~~~ sdparse
A fost spânzurat . \n Has been hanged . 
auxpass(spânzurat, fost)
~~~
