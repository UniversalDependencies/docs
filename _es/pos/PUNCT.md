### Definition

Punctuation marks are non-alphabetical characters and character groups used in many languages to delimit linguistic units in printed text.

Punctuation is not taken to include logograms such as _$_, _%_, and _ยง_, which are instead tagged as [SYM]().

### Examples

- Period: _<b>.</b>_
- Comma: _<b>,</b>_
- Parentheses: _<b>()</b>_
