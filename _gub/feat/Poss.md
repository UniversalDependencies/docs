`Poss`

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Human

The feature `Poss` is similar to the feature [`Rel`](https://github.com/UniversalDependencies/docs/blob/pages-source/_tpn/feat/Rel.md), but it indicates that the dependent of the head - possessor in case of a noun -- is incorporated to the head.
#### Examples

* _<b>aʔɨr</b>&nbsp;_ “Son”
* _<b>he-r-aʔɨr</b>&nbsp;_ “My son” (1.POSS-Cont-son)

* _<b>ukaz</b>&nbsp;_ “trap”
* _<b>nerukaz</b>&nbsp;_ “your trap” (2.POSS-Cont-hand)
