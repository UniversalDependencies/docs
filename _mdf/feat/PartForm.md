Moksha has multiple participles forms, here termed the: Prs = Y, NegConvPrc = apak, Past = F.

### <a name="NegConvPrc">`NegConvPrc`</a>: negative converb participle (апак)

The Apak participle, negative converb participle, is usually followed by a connegative form
in the verbal adverb and verbal adjective functions. Additionally, it may be followed by
the VGen or PastTemp participles.

#### Examples

* [mdf] _ Ну, кли онцтон, пара: гулянять пацяняц <b>апак</b> синтть._ "Well, it was in my dream, good: the little pigeon's wing isn't broken." (negative converb participle)

### <a name="Prs">`Prs`</a>: present participle 

The Y or present participle is a homonym of the indicative present third person singular verb form.

#### Examples

* [mdf] _<b>лакыця</b> ведь_ "<b>boiling</b> water" (present participle)


### <a name="Past">`Past`</a>: past participle 

The F or past participle may be seen as a past or perfect participle used with transitive and intransitive verbs alike.

#### Examples

* [mdf] _Тон, улема, ичкозде <b>сафат</b>?_ "You, apparently, <b>have/are come</b> from a long ways away." (past participle)

* [mdf] _<b>праф</b> лопа_ "a <b>fallen</b> leaf" (past participle)
