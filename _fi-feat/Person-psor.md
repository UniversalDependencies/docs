### `1`: first person

Examples:

* [fi] _<b>tekojeni</b> seurakset_ "the consequences of <b>my actions</b>"

### `2`: second person

Examples:

* [fi] _<b>tekojesi</b> seuraukset_ "the consequences of <b>your actions</b>"

### `3`: third person

Examples:

* [fi] _<b>tekojensa</b> seuraukset_ "the consequences of <b>their actions</b>"
