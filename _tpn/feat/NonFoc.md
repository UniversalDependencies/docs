`NFoc` refers to a construction in which and adverbial adjunct is fronted, becoming focalized. The fronting of the adverbial constituent triggers nominalization of the main predicate which, in this, case, is non-focal (NonFoc). In Tupí-Guaraní language descritpions, this construction is usually called indicative-II or oblique topicalized.


### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes



#### Examples

* _<b>Kori i-juká-w</b>&nbsp;_ “ Today he killed him ” (Anch., Arte, 3 9v). Compare _o-(i)-juká kori_ "He killed him today".
