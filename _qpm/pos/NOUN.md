## NOUN

### Definition

Nouns are a part of speech typically denoting a person, place, thing, animal or idea.

The `NOUN` tag is intended for common nouns only. 


#### Examples

- _mómiče_ "[a] girl"
- _kóte_ "[a] cat"
- _góram_ "[a] tree"
- _vǽtar_ "air / wind"
- _kamatniná_ "beauty"
- _hódeņe_ "going"
- _gováņe_ "fasting"
- _dúmeņe_ "speaking"
- _varvéņe_ "walking"
- _spaņé_ "sleep"
