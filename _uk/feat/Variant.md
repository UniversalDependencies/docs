Sometimes there are multiple word forms for the same lemma and set of features.
The `Variant` feature helps to distinguish the alternate forms.

### <a name="Short">`Short`</a>: short form of adjectives

#### Examples

* _<b>здатен</b>_ (instead of _здатний_) “able”
* _<b>потрібен</b>_ (instead of _потрібний_) “necessary”
* _<b>кожен</b>_ (instead of _кожний_) “everyone”


### <a name="Uncontr">`Uncontr`</a>: uncontracted form of adjectives

#### Examples

* _<b>бідная</b>_ (instead of _бідна_) “poor”
