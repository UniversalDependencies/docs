compound: compound

compound in Italian is used for noun compounds and numbers (e.g. numbers expressed alphabetically).

Examples:

gli uffici leva
compound(uffici, leva)

tra donne deputate
compound(donne, deputate)

la parola chiave
compound(parola, chiave)

Oggi la 10 km donne
compound(km, donne)
det(km, la)
nummod(km, 10)

200 mila
compound(mila, 200)
