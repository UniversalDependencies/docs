A determiner is the relation between the head of a nominal phrase and its determiner. 

~~~ sdparse
Den engelska parken är vacker \n The English park is beautiful
det(parken, Den)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Vilken bok föredrar du ? \n Which book do you prefer ?
det(bok, Vilken)
~~~
