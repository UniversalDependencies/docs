### Definition

Adposition is a cover term for prepositions and postpositions. 
Beja has mainly postpositions. 
They are independent or enclitic. Some of the enclitics agree in number with the noun they complete.
They can be treated as case markers.

### Examples 

- _baːji_ "towards"
- _=eːb_ (locative)
- _haːj_ (comitative)
- _=ka_ (distributive)
- _suːr_ "before"
