The tag `VERB` covers PTB tags VB, VBP, VBZ, VBD, VBG, VBN, except for auxiliary verb uses of _be_, _have_, _do_, and _get_. 

(Auxiliary verbs and modals are `AUX` and the infinitive _to_ is `PART`.)
