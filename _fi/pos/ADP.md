Adposition is a cover term for prepositions and postpositions.

#### Examples

* [fi] _alla_ "under", _päällä_ "over"
* [fi] _ennen_ "before", _jälkeen_ "after"
* [fi] _kanssa_ "with", _ilman_ "without"
