### <a name="Inf">`Inf`</a>: infinitive marker

#### Examples

* _<b>zu</b>_

### <a name="Res">`Res`</a>: response particle

Called particles in the German grammar, they are tagged as [interjections](/u/pos/INTJ.html) following the UD guidelines.

#### Examples

* _<b>ja</b>_ “yes”
* _<b>nein</b>_ “no”

### <a name="Vbp">`Vbp`</a>: separated verb prefix

They are analogous to verbal particles in other Germanic languages,
which again overlap with adpositions and adverbs. Do we want to tag
them as adpositions/adverbs and add this feature?

#### Examples

* _<b>vor</b>_ (in _stellen Sie sich vor_, lit. “-magine you i-”)
