`Excl` is defined in Tekó as particle _`-sipo`_, which both has interogative and exclamative features.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes


#### Examples


* _owa, emenate<b>sipo</b> idʒe aʔu&nbsp;_ “Oh, it's my husband who I ate.”
