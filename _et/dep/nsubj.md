The dependency type `nsubj` marks nominal subjects of the non-copular clause.

~~~ sdparse
Kass nägi koera . \n The cat saw the dog .
nsubj(nägi-2, Kass-1)
obj(nägi-2, koera-3)
punct(nägi-2, .-4)
nsubj(saw,cat)
~~~
