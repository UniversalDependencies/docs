### `Coll`: word is colloquial

#### Examples

* [fi] _et_ "that" (colloquial form of _että_)
* [fi] _mut_ "but" (colloquial form of _mutta_)

### `Arch`: archaic, obsolete

#### Examples

* [fi] _wanha_ "old" (archaic form of _vanha_)
