This is a subtype of [aux](), used for the necessative auxiliaries and particles _ковны, должен_.

The necessative auxiliary, when attached to a predicate,
typically carries some of the tense/aspect/modality suffixes as well as person/number agreement suffixes.


~~~ sdparse
Сылы коліс юрсисӧ мичаа вӧчны ,... \n She needed to do her hair in a more beautiful way,...
obl:agent(вӧчны-5, Сылы-1)
aux:nec(вӧчны-5, коліс-2)
obj(вӧчны-5, юрсисӧ-3)
advmod(вӧчны-5, мичаа-4)
~~~
