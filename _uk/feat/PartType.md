Types of particles are found in various tagsets and are highly language-specific.

### <a name="Conseq">`Conseq`</a>: particle of consequence

The particle _то_ is used in the beginning of the main clause that follows a conditional subordinate
clause. It introduces the consequence of the condition. (A possible alternative analysis would be
that the particle is a conjunction.)

#### Examples

* _Бо коли чоловік робить лише добрі діла, <b>то</b> його писання теж є ділом на пожиток людям.&nbsp;_
  “For when a man does only good deeds, <b>(then)</b> his writing is also a work for the benefit of people.”
