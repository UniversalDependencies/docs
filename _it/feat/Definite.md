The feature `Definite` is marked to identify definite and indefinite [articles](u-pos/DET).  

### `Ind`: indefinite

#### Examples

* _<b>un</b> cane_ "a dog"
* _<b>una</b> bella giornata_ "a good day"

### `Def`: definite

#### Examples

* _<b>il</b> cane_ "the dog"
* _<b>gli</b> altri_ "others"

### `Red`: reduced

Not used.

### `Com`: complex

Not used.
