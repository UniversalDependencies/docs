NumForm: numeral form

Feature of cardinal and ordinal numbers. Is the number expressed by digits or as a word?

Word: number expressed as word

Examples

  • jeden “one”, dva “two”, tři “three”

Digit: number expressed using digits

Examples

  • 1, 2, 3

Roman: roman numeral

Examples

  • I, II, III
