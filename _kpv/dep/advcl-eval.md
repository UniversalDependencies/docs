The _advcl:eval_ relation is used for evaluative adverbal clauses.
See also `advmod:eval`.

~~~ sdparse
... войнас, тыдалӧ, тэрмасьӧмӧн сӧвтӧмаӧсь да нуӧмаӧсь сэсь турунсӧ. \n ... at night, it appears, the hay had been loaded and mowed here in a hurry.
advcl:eval(сӧвтӧмаӧсь, тыдалӧ)
advcl:eval(loaded, it_appears)


~~~
