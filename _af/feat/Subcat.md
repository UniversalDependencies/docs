In Afrikaans, we label transitivity with this feature.


### <a name="Intr">`Intr`</a>: intransitive verb

This feature is used for an intransitive verb.

#### Examples

* _'n Verwysingsnommer sal op die skerm <b>verskyn</b> sodra jy die vorm ingedien het._ “A reference number will appear on the screen as soon as you submitted the form.”


### <a name="Prep">`Prep`</a>: verbs with preposition

This feature is used for a separable verb where the preposition has been separated and for verbs that go with a prepositional object.

#### Examples

* _In daardie geval beveel ons aan dat jy dit na jou rekenaar aflaai en liewer van daar af oopmaak._ “In that case, we recommend you download it to your computer and rather open it over there.”


### <a name="Tran">`Tran`</a>: transitive verb

This feature is used for a transitive verb.

#### Examples

* _Hierdie dienste <b>sluit</b> water, elektrisiteit, sanitasie en vullisverwydering in._ “These services include water, electricity, sanitation and garbage collection.”
