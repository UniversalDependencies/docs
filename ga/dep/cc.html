cc: coordinating conjunction

The cc' label markes the relation between the first conjunct of a coordinated phrase and its coordinating conjunction. In Irish, a coordinating conjunction takes the form of _agus_ and’, nó or', _ná_ than’. UD takes the first conjunct as the head of a coordinated phrase. (Note that this differs from the Irish Dependency Treebank structure, where the coordinating conjunction is the head of the cooordinated phrase.)

caidreamh a bhunú le bainc cheannais agus le hinstitiúidí airgeadais i dtíortha eile to establish a relationship between central banks and financial institutions in other countries.
