### Definition

Adverbs are words that typically modify [verbs](VERB), [nouns](NOUN) or other [adverbs](ADV).

### Examples

- _Umeʔegatu <b> zekaipo</b> tukaz rehe_ "<b>It is said</b> that it stared at the trap"
- _arapuha roʔo <b>kwer</b>_ "venison (deer-meat-<b>PAST</b>)"
