This relation is a subtype of the [nmod]() relation, which captures cases where something syntactically a noun phrase is
used as an adverbial modifier in a sentence; This subtype has been **deprecated** by the UD Core Group and has been superceded by `obl:unmarked` since UD v2.15.

~~~ sdparse
ה חצר עצמה ו ה מנזרים סביבה מהווים חלק אינטגרלי מ ה כנסייה
nmod:npmod(חצר, עצמה)
~~~
