`Also` is a clitic which may be literally translated as 'also', 'true'. It is often found in sentences with coorditation.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes


#### Examples

* _zawara<b>we</b>&nbsp; oekaruwe;_ “And the dog also looks.”
