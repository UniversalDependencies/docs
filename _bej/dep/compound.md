The `compound` relation is one of three relations for multiword expressions (MWEs) (the other two being [fixed]() and [flat]()).

~~~ sdparse
a- gar jʔ -an =t i= gaw =i /
text_en: I went back, in my house.
compound(jʔ, gar) ("come", "come_back")
~~~
