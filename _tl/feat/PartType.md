Types of particles are found in various tagsets and are highly language-specific.

### <a name="Int">`Int`</a>: question particle

#### Examples

* _Umuulan <b>ba</b>?_ “Is it raining?”

### <a name="Nfh">`Nfh`</a>: non-first hand

#### Examples

* _Darating <b>daw</b> si Pedro bukas._ “<b>They say</b> Pedro will arrive tomorrow.”

### <a name="Des">`Des`</a>: desiderative

#### Examples

* _Mabuti <b>sana</b> ang ani._ “<b>I hope</b> the harvest is good.”
