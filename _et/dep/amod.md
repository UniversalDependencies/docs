An adjectival modifier of a noun is any adjectival phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the noun.

[et]  _Ta kannab <b>valgeid</b> sokke_ "She is wearing <b>white</b> socks"

~~~ sdparse
Ta kannab valgeid sokke \n She is wearing white socks
amod(sokke, valgeid)
~~~
