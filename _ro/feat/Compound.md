`Compound` is a boolean feature that marks prepositions and conjunctions that can be
etymologically explained as fusions of multiple words, e.g., _despre = de + spre_.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: fused word

#### Examples

* _<b>din</b>_ “from”
* _<b>pentru</b>_ “for”
* _<b>după</b>_ “after”
* _<b>prin</b>_ “through”
