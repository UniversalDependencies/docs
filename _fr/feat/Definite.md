In French, the `Definite` feature is marked only on the [determiners](en-pos/DET).

### <a name="Ind">`Ind`</a>: indefinite

#### Examples

* _un, une_

### <a name="Def">`Def`</a>: definite

#### Examples

* _le, la, les_
