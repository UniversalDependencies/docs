advmod: adverbial modifier

The dependency type advmod is used for adverbial modifiers of verbs, nominal verbs, adjectives and nominal adjectives.

ゆっくり 走る 。 \n slowly run .
advmod(走る, ゆっくり)
punct(走る, 。)
