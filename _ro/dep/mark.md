title: 'mark'
shortdef: 'marker'
---

A marker is the word introducing a finite clause subordinate to another clause:

~~~sdparse
Văd că plouă . \n See-I that rains .
mark(plouă, că)
~~~

~~~sdparse
Vin , dacă pot . \n Come-I , if can-I .
mark(pot, dacă)
~~~
