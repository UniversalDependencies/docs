Definite: definiteness or state

The feature Definite is marked to identify definite and indefinite articles.

Ind: indefinite

Examples

  • un cane “a dog”
  • una bella giornata “a good day”

Def: definite

Examples

  • il cane “the dog”
  • gli altri “others”

Red: reduced

Not used.

Com: complex

Not used.
