The `obl` relation is used for a nominal (noun, pronoun, noun phrase) functioning as a non-core (oblique) argument or adjunct. This means that it functionally corresponds to an adverbial attaching to a verb, adjective or other adverb.

- temporal nominal modifiers:

~~~ sdparse
Валя цяла нощ . \n Rained-it all night .
nmod(Валя, нощ)
nmod(Rained-it, night)
~~~

- predicate modification:

~~~ sdparse
От всички маси ги следяха враждебни погледи . \n From all tables them.CLITIC followed hostile gazes  .
nmod(следяха, маси)
nmod(followed, tables)
~~~
