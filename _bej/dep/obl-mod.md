In Beja-NSC treebank, we use the `obl:mod` relation for nominal adjuncts.

~~~ sdparse
da / bʔeː =b eːjaː -n =heːb i- ni //
text_en: Men came to me one day", he said.
obl:mod(eːjaː, bʔeː) ("come", "day")
~~~
