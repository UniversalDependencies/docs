The category `acl` is used for adverbial modification of a noun, but not for modification of a predicate; for the latter case, see [advcl]().

~~~ sdparse
POJKE SITTA VARELSE-Vb-BEFINNA-SIG LEDSEN \n The-boy sat (there), sad
acl(POJKE, LEDSEN)
~~~
