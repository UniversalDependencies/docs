In Tekó, the particle _`-te`_ is used to indicate focus.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

#### Examples

* _zotunaʔɨɲa<b>te</b> wane poroiruwo_&nbsp; “It is the heart that gives life.”
